# NEC 2023 - National Electrical Code Index

## Overview

The National Electrical Code (NFPA 70) 2023 edition is the primary standard for electrical installation in the United States. This index provides quick reference to key articles relevant to AE/MEP design and QA/QC validation.

## Document Organization

The NEC is organized into:
- **Introduction**: How to use the code
- **Articles 1-9 (Chapters 1-9)**: Main requirements organized by topic
- **Annexes A-J**: Informational material (not enforceable unless specifically adopted)

## Adoption and Amendments

The NEC is typically adopted at the state/local level with:
- **Adoption lag**: 1-3 years after publication (2023 NEC may be adopted 2024-2026)
- **Amendments**: States/cities may modify or add requirements
- **Effective date**: Varies by jurisdiction

**Always verify the adopted edition and any amendments with the Authority Having Jurisdiction (AHJ)**.
